I am currently a postgraduate of computer science at University of Science and Technology of China. I major in data mining at Anhui Province Key Laboratory of Big Data Analysis and Application and my tutor is Prof. Qi Liu.

My main research area is the data mining on the online education system and my research interests include Deep Learning (DL), Knowledge Graph (KG), Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Reinforcement Learning (RL). By now, I already have 12 papers published in KDD, AAAI, IJCAI, SIGIR, ICDM and so on. At the same time, I serve as the PC member of IJCAI2021, IJCAI2022. Also, I have 8 public patents and one is under licensing announcement. I am in charge of several open source projects related to online education system: EduData, EduCDM, EduKTM, EduNLP, EduSim, EduRec. I also develop a toolkit framework to quickly construct different architectures for different purposes, named longling, which is still in progress. Moreover, I am responsible for constructing education knowledge graphs LUNA for my laboratory.
