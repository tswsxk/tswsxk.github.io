Shiwei Tong

I am currently a postgraduate of computer science at University of Science and Technology of China. I major in data mining at Anhui Province Key Laboratory of Big Data Analysis and Application and my tutor is Dr. Qi Liu.

My research interests include Deep Learning (DL), Knowledge Graph (KG), Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Reinforcement Learning (RL). By now, I already have two papers published in AAAI'19 and KDD'19, and one related to NLP is under review. Also, I have one received patent and two are under review. I also develop a toolkit framework to quickly construct different architectures for different purposes, named longling, which is still in progress. Recently, I am responsible for constructing knowledge graphs for my laboratory.

I am familiar with python, and know the basic grammar of C, java, html, javascript. I grasp the basic usage of some common frameworks for development like webpack, vue in frontend, flask in backend and beautifulsoup for crawling. I have some experience on managing servers (CentOS) and database, knowing the basic usage of mysql, neo4j, mongodb, and elasticsearch. In addition, I know git, kafka, thrift and used spark and hive but be out of practice currently. My most favorite machine learning frameworks are sklearn and mxnet.
